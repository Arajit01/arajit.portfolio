Privacy Commitment

Your trust is the foundation of my creative process. Here is how I handle your data.

1. Data Collection

When you interact with my portfolio—whether through the contact form, booking system, or AI Assistant—I collect specific information to serve you better:

  • Identity Data: Full name and professional email address.
  • Project Context: Design briefs, brand guidelines, and project files shared during consultations.
  • Communication Logs: Messages sent via the AI Chatbot and EmailJS integration.

2. Information Security

I implement industry-standard security measures to ensure your data remains confidential. I do not sell, trade, or leak your personal details to marketing agencies. Your project data is stored securely using Google Cloud Infrastructure via Google Apps Script.

3. Portfolio & Confidentiality

By default, I showcase completed projects in my portfolio to demonstrate my expertise. However, I deeply respect business confidentiality. If your project requires an NDA or you prefer it not to be displayed publicly, please notify me during the booking phase.

4. Third-Party Integrations

To provide a seamless experience, this site uses:

  • EmailJS: For reliable contact form delivery.
  • Google Apps Script: For automated booking and database management.
  • Custom AI: To provide instant support and project guidance.
