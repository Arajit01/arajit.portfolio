Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common inquiries about my design process and services.

My process starts with a discovery phase to understand your brand goals. I then create 2-3 initial concepts, refine the chosen direction based on your feedback, and deliver the final polished assets.

Standard assets like a single logo or poster usually take 3-5 business days. Larger branding systems or UI layouts typically take 1-2 weeks depending on complexity.

You will receive high-resolution, industry-standard files including AI, EPS, SVG (vector), PDF, and transparent PNG/JPG for both web and print use.

I offer a full refund if cancelled before work begins. Partial refunds are available during the concept phase, but no refunds are issued once final files are delivered.
